About Us

Kalauba Trading and Projects is a 100% black woman owned company and is an innovative brand founded to provide IT and Telecommunocations services and solutions that are meant to assist our customers to provide simple and effective products to the end user KALAUBA TRADING AND PROJECTS Consulting is committed to developing middleware solutions to help customers intergrate their traditional systems and achieve an intergrated network which will produce results in a more real-time and automatic manner.

Mission

To be a service provider in this day in age that delivers the right service, at the right price and making a profit. By providing our customers with business solutions that will help them run their businesses with the highest level of agility, simplicity and efficiency.

Vision

Kalauba Trading and Projects Consulting seeks to be the primary driver of new technological solutions in Africa and to enhance the way business is done in technologically underprivileged countries of our continent.
